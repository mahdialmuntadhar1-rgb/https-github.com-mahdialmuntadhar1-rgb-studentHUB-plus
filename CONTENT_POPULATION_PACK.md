# Content Population Pack

This pack prepares Jamiaati for richer public content without publishing unchecked content immediately.

Included:
- trusted jobs source pack
- trusted scholarship source pack
- Iraqi university news/event source pack
- evergreen scholarship cards
- starter campus-life/social demo posts

Safety:
- new content is inserted as pending_review
- public feed will not show it until approved
- no real email is sent
- no deployment is triggered
- existing public app remains stable
